Due to your provided input under the last generated section we have compiled the following trip for you to consider. Level of Climbing

Most of the climbs at Index consist of 5-9 to 5.12c climbs. Matching somewhat inline with the input you entered climbs should be moderate to difficult in this region.

Type of Rock

Found in Index is mostly granite and limestone. This rock makes for excellent grip in colder weathers and cuts up the hands. Make sure to bring along climbers tape and grippy shoes. s

Directions to the Trailhead

Coming from Seattle, WA take I-5 North until reaching the Woodinville exit 36. Take the exit and continue until connecting with Highway 2 across the Cascades. Following this road the Index turnoff will be reached in 30 minutes.
