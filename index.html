Project Description

Learning new skills is hard, we are here to help you out. In a world where everyone has something more important to do than learn something new, it can be hard to commit time to build up a trait you donâ€™t have a particular need for. People who use our platform will better themselves by understanding how, why and when they decide to dedicate time to gain expertise in a new hobby.

People care about our product because we are helping better themselves. By understanding motivations behind how communities organize their time for practicing a hobby we can reach conclusions on how to improve their personal growth. We do this by effectively curating an experience that compiles and displays user learning behavior over the course of a month.
