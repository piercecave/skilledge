SkillEdge





In a world with infinite demand, it can be diffcult to commit time to learn something new. Our attention gets scattered and diffused.


SkillEdge! solves this issue by combining industry research and organizational techniques to make learning your new skill achievable,productive, and fun.





Features



Calendar

Allows you to quickly see your progress over time."You can click on it!"


Charts

Personalized visualizations to track your progress and learn from your mistakes and sucesses!


Avatars

Helpful animals to help cheer you along!
